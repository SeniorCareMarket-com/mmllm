gate weights and output norms agree on which tier each layer wants.

### Gap analysis

#### What is *still* missing

**Net's V in L1/L2/L3 became useless.** ‖net_out‖ in those layers
dropped to ~0.6–1.5 (vs Local's 34–53). Net is contributing only via L0;
the other three layers have routed it to ~0 gate weight AND its output
norm has shrunk. That's not specialization, that's underutilization.
The architecture has Net structurally available in every layer but only
one layer found it useful.

**Δ_net plateaus at 0.065 rather than rising.** The plateau is healthy
in the sense that it doesn't collapse, but the brief's "target sustained
> 0.10" wasn't met in absolute terms — Δ_net stayed under 0.10 from
step 750 onward. Net's L0 specialist contribution is bounded; without
L1/L2/L3 helping, Δ_net can't exceed what one layer's Net adds.

#### Persistent failure mode + concrete next test

If the headline metric is "every layer uses Net non-trivially," this run
still misses it — L1/L2/L3 Net is silent. The mechanism is that the
3-way gate softmax + Bernoulli combination lets the gate drive w_net to
0 with no penalty, and once w_net=0 there's no gradient flowing back to
Net's V in that layer, so Net's V collapses (visible in ‖net_out‖
shrinking).

**Single-knob fix to test next: `MMLLM_GATE_W_NET_FLOOR=0.05`** —
post-softmax floor on w_net per layer. Renormalize the other two
weights down to fit. This forces every layer to put at least 5% of its
attention-mixing budget through Net, which means Net's V receives
gradient in every layer (instead of just L0), and ‖net_out‖ in L1/L2/L3
can't decay to noise.

**Telemetry that would confirm**: ‖net_out‖ in L1/L2/L3 should stay
non-negligible (say > 5) instead of collapsing to <2. Δ_net should rise
above 0.10 because all 4 layers contribute meaningful Net signal, not
just L0. The risk is that forcing a floor recreates the destructive
interference we just fixed — so the floor needs to be small (0.05 is a
reasonable first try; if that breaks training, try 0.02).

#### Honest take

This is the cleanest, most stable single-worker run of any round so far.
Both prior failure modes are gone, both bank tiers are contributing,
synergy is consistent across the whole run, no spikes, no decay. The
architectural fix-pile (Bernoulli gate + residual target + direction-only
+ low LR_DENSE) works as a coherent system.

But "stable Δ_net = 0.065" is what we got, and the brief targeted
"Δ_net > 0.10 sustained." The mechanism for not hitting that target is
Net being silent in 3 of 4 layers. A gate floor on w_net is the
narrowest knob change that would force the issue. If 5 workers in this
configuration all hit similar plateaus around 0.06–0.07, then the
harvest's job is to test whether the per-layer specializations *across
different families* compose into a model where every layer does
something useful — which would also enlarge Δ_net mechanically.
